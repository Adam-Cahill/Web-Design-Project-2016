I am mainly looking foward to Horizon Zero Dawn as I don't think that I have played anything like it before and so I am looking foward to doing something new and trying to different techniques in taking something out.

The plot of Horizon Zero Dawn revolves around Aloy, a hunter and archer who lives in a world overrun by robots. Having been cloistered her whole life, she sets out to discover the dangers that kept her sheltered for so long. The character makes use of ranged, melee weapons and stealth tactics to combat the mechanised creatures whose remains can also be looted for resources. The game features an open world environment for Aloy to explore, divided into tribes that hold side quests to accomplish while the main story guides her throughout the whole world.
